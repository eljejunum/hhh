Ever thought about completing a 5k walk/run? Fear might have gotten the best of you OR maybe you didn’t have the right support. On October 5th Hilton Head Health is hosting the 4th annual Devin’s Dash Memorial 5k event. For many, this is the first 5k they’ll complete and the inspiration they need to continue living a healthy lifestyle. Whether you are a first-timer or a veteran the scenic 3.1 mile jaunt through Shipyard Plantation will leave you feeling invigorated and accomplished. Join us the week of September 29th – October 6th for exciting program additions that culminate with the event of the year!

Romila “Dr. Romie” Mushtaq’s journey has taken her from helping patients to experiencing their fears due to her own life-threatening illness. Along the way, she’s learned the importance of the mind-body connection in healing. In her powerful lecture, “Empower Your Brain, Transform Your Body”, Dr. Romie shares warm and revealing stories of personal growth, mindful lifestyle change, and the unique ways neuroscience and spirituality can work together to promote the healing process.
